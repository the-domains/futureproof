HR Viewpoints – Benefits fatigue and how to combat it

One of the biggest risks for employers with generous or engaging benefits schemes is employees getting benefit fatigue - in other words, so used to the benefits they fail to value them any longer. Andrew Drake, head of reward & benefits at JLT Employee Benefits, likens this to the ceaseless development of the Apple iPhone.
